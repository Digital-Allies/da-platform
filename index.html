Frequently Asked Questions

Real questions. Straight answers. No fine print.

What exactly does Digital Allies do? +
I design, build, connect, and maintain technology for small businesses and nonprofits. That starts with design — websites, brand identity, menus, signage, pitch decks, and graphic assets — then extends into development, automations, integrations between your apps, SEO/AEO audits, and ongoing tech support. Think of it as a design-forward consultancy that also handles the technical wiring.
Is the consulting really free? +
Yes. Tech consulting, AI consulting, brand discovery, design consultations, and AEO/SEO reviews are all free. I do not charge for conversations or clarity. You only pay when I design or build something. All costs are quoted before work begins.
What is the No-Ghosting Guarantee? +
If I take your project, I finish it. If something changes, I tell you directly. Going quiet mid-project is not part of my service model. You will always know where things stand.
Do you work with businesses outside of Kingman? +
Absolutely. I am based in Kingman, Arizona, but most of the work I do is digital. I serve clients locally and remotely. If you have an internet connection and a problem worth solving, we can work together.
Do you do design work or just development? +
Design comes first. I build websites, brand identities, menus, signage, social graphics, pitch decks — polished print and digital visuals that look like you meant it. Brand discovery sessions are free. Once the visual direction is locked in, I develop the technology to match. Design-forward consultancy that also handles the technical wiring.
What does AEO mean and why should I care? +
AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimization. It is about structuring your content so AI assistants — like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Siri — can find and recommend your business. SEO gets you on the search results page. AEO gets you into the AI's answer.
