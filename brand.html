1. Grid Architecture & Symmetry Break

Aesthetic symmetry serves as a visual proxy for technical competence. We construct layouts on a rigid 12-column grid to prove structural discipline. However, to guide focus, we employ the Symmetry Break strategy: we nudge a single high-contrast highlight exactly 5px to 10px off-center to create a calculated visual itch that draws the human eye directly to our objective.

The Technical Lace Spec

Our canvas is Bone White (#F9F6F0). Grid spacing is locked at 20px, with 0.5px solid borders in #E0DDD5 mapping horizontal and vertical axes.

Extreme Whitespace

We mandate twice the whitespace of standard web templates. Generous margin and padding rules signal confidence, allowing high-contrast points of interest to breathe.

Visual Itch in Practice

By shifting the red indicator dot exactly 8px to the right, we break perfect alignment. The subconscious immediately registers the asymmetry, pulling visual focus effortlessly.

2. Color System: The 95/5 Rule

To ensure signals are absolute, we restrict color usage to a 95/5 split. Ninety-five percent of our layout remains purely neutral (Bone White and Charcoal), serving as the quiet frame that empowers the five percent colored alerts (Pulse Blue, Signal Red, or Light Pink highlights) to trigger immediate, non-competitive reactions.

3. Typography Hierarchy

Typefaces are selected to eliminate cognitive load in high-glare, mobile environments. Lexend Deca is our heading font, while JetBrains Mono handles technical details, pricing, and system parameters.

Lexend Deca · Headings & Action Cues

Aa Bb Cc

Designed with expanded breathing room and high x-height to maximize scannability. Used primarily for titles, subtitles, card headers, and button callouts.
