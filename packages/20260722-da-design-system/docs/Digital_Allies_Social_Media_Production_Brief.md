# Digital Allies — Social Media Production Brief

---

## Brand Identity
- **Brand**: Digital Allies
- **Founded**: 2024
- **Location**: Kingman, Arizona
- **Founder**: Anthony
- **Website**: digitalallies.net
- **Tagline**: "Technological solutions for people with better things to do"
- **Core Voice**: Clean engineering, clear communication, and follow-through that won't require follow-up
- **Tone Coordinates**: 65% Formal, 55% Dry, 70% Technical, 75% Confident
- **Philosophy**: Eliminates corporate jargon in favor of straight-talking language. No forced humor. No corporate buzzwords. Clear, warm, professional, human.

---

## Color Palette (95/5 Rule — 95% neutral, 5% accent)

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Bone White | #F9F6F0 | Primary light background (95%) |
| Charcoal Grey | #2D2D2D | Primary dark background, text, lines |
| Pulse Blue | #3A7BD5 | Primary accent — logo dot, headlines, CTAs. NEVER as large fill |
| Signal Red | #C5301A | Secondary accent — emphasis, alert markers, logo variant |
| Light Pink | #FADEEB | Tertiary — subtle warmth, very rare use |

**Color Rules:**
- Light cards: Bone White #F9F6F0 background, Charcoal text, Blue or Red accent on ONE word/phrase per headline only
- Dark cards: Charcoal #2D2D2D background, white text, Blue or Red accent on ONE word/phrase per headline only
- Carousel rhythm: Alternate light and dark cards for visual rhythm
- Only one accent color per headline — the rest stays white (dark bg) or charcoal (light bg)
- Pink/salmon can substitute for red on light backgrounds to soften contrast
- NEVER use Pulse Blue as a large background fill — it weakens its impact as an accent

## Typography

| Font | Weight | Usage |
|------|--------|-------|
| Lexend Deca | Bold/Black | Headlines, titles, action cues, primary messaging |
| Lexend Deca | Regular | Eyebrow labels, category text (always ALL-CAPS, tracked out) |
| JetBrains Mono | Regular | Body text, descriptions, footnotes, source citations, technical details, pricing |

**Typography Rules:**
- Headlines: Bold geometric Lexend Deca, large, impactful
- Meta-labels: ALL-CAPS Lexend Deca, tracked out, small (e.g., "PLAIN TALK", "REAL KINGMAN DATA", "DIGITAL STRATEGY")
- Body/data: JetBrains Mono monospace — gives a "data report" / technical feel
- Category labels sit exactly one grid-unit above the headline
- No script fonts, no serif fonts, no decorative type — ever

---

## Grid System
- 12-column rigid grid
- **"Technical Lace" background**: subtle graph-paper grid visible on EVERY card — very fine hairline perpendicular lines layered beneath all content
- **"Symmetry Break" strategy**: 5–10px offset on one element per card for visual focus
- Double standard whitespace (macro-padding) — generous breathing room signals confidence
- All borders: thin (1–2px), sharp 90-degree corners, NO rounded corners

---

## Layout Template (Every Card)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│ [●] Digital Allies    CATEGORY  │  ← Header: Logo top-left, category label top-right
│                                 │
│                                 │
│         MAIN CONTENT            │  ← Body: Headline + supporting text
│                                 │
│                                 │
│                        [04/08]  │  ← Footer: Page counter bottom-right in thin stroke box
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Header Zone:**
- Blue dot (●) in #3A7BD5 (or #C5301A on red-accented slides) + "Digital Allies" wordmark in Lexend Deca, anchored top-left
- Category/series label anchored top-right in ALL-CAPS Lexend Deca Regular, tracked out (e.g., "PLAIN TALK", "REAL KINGMAN DATA", "DIGITAL STRATEGY")
- Header sits within the top grid row with generous horizontal padding

**Body Zone:**
- Main content area spans center of card
- Headlines in Lexend Deca Bold — large, impactful, with ONE word/phrase in accent color
- Descriptions, data, and supporting copy in JetBrains Mono Regular
- Content framed in thin-bordered (1px) rectangular boxes where needed to isolate data points or call-outs
- No rounded corners on any box

**Footer Zone:**
- Carousel page counter (e.g., "04 / 08") enclosed in a thin-stroke rectangular box, bottom-right corner
- Always present on carousel posts
- Font: JetBrains Mono, small, charcoal or white depending on card background

## Logo Usage
- Primary: Blue dot (#3A7BD5) + "Digital Allies" in Lexend Deca — always top-left of every card
- The dot can be blue OR red depending on the card's accent color

## Content Types to Generate

### 1. Educational / Data Insights
**Series label:** `REAL KINGMAN DATA`
- Market statistics, local Kingman AZ business data, SEO/AEO explainers
- Use thin-bordered data boxes to isolate stats
- Source citations in JetBrains Mono small text, bottom of content area
- Example hook: "Only 12% of Kingman businesses have a mobile-optimized site."

### 2. Tactical Tips
**Series label:** `PLAIN TALK`
- Numbered tips (01, 02, 03…) with actionable, jargon-free advice
- One tip per slide in a carousel, or a tight list of 3–5 tips on a single card
- Tip numbers in large Lexend Deca, accented in blue or red
- Example: "01 — Your contact form should have fewer fields than a parking ticket."

### 3. Engagement / Interactive
**Series label:** `YOUR TURN`
- Questions to the audience, poll prompts, reaction invitations
- Single bold question as headline, no supporting copy needed
- Footer counter still present; CTA copy replaces page number on final slide
- Example: "What's the one thing on your website you've been meaning to fix for 6 months?"

### 4. Brand Story / CTA
**Series label:** `ABOUT THIS`
- "Why I built this," "Book a Discovery Call" action posts
- First-person, grounded, no hype
- CTA slide: bold action headline + URL (digitalallies.net) in JetBrains Mono
- Example: "I started Digital Allies because small businesses deserve the same tools big ones pay consultants $400/hour for."

### 5. Thought Leadership
**Series label:** `DIGITAL STRATEGY`
- Synthesis of data, industry observations, hot takes in plain language
- Can include a simple diagram or icon (see Icon System)
- Example: "AEO isn't replacing SEO. It's what SEO always should have been."

## Carousel Structure

- **Total slides:** 6–10 per carousel
- **Slide 1 (Hook):** Dark Charcoal (#2D2D2D) background. Bold white headline with ONE accent-colored word. Series label top-right. No page counter on Slide 1 (or show "01 / 08").
- **Slides 2–N (Content):** Alternating light (Bone White) and dark (Charcoal) cards. Each slide advances the argument, tip, or data set.
- **Final Slide (CTA/Engagement):** Dark background preferred. Bold CTA or question. "Book a call → digitalallies.net" in JetBrains Mono. Page counter shows final number.
- Every slide: header (logo + category) + footer (page counter) — no exceptions
- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1 square — 1080×1080px for Instagram and LinkedIn

## Voice Guidelines

- **First person "I"** — personal accountability, not corporate "we"
- Straight-talking, no jargon. If a complex term is needed, define it plainly in the next line
- Confident but not arrogant. Helpful but not salesy
- Dry wit is acceptable; forced humor is not
- **Good example:** "Your website shouldn't need a manual. If it does, something went wrong."
- **Bad example:** "We leverage cutting-edge synergies to optimize your digital ecosystem."

|------------------|---------------|

| Scalable | It won't break if more than three people visit |

| Ecosystem | The pile of apps you've subscribed to |

| Pivot | Change |
| Circle back | Reply |
| Deliverable | The thing you're getting |

| Icon | Represents |

## What to AVOID — Hard Rules

| ❌ Never Do This | ✅ Do This Instead |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Photography | Pure graphic design only |
| Emoji or cartoon icons | Diagram-style line art icons |
| Rounded corners on boxes/borders | Sharp 90-degree corners only |
| Corporate jargon or forced humor | Plain, direct, dry language |
| Gradients or drop shadows | Flat, clean, no depth effects |
| "Digital Allies \| 2026" footer credits | Logo in header only |
| Large color fills in Pulse Blue | Surgical accent — one word/phrase only |
| More than ONE accent-colored word per headline | Pick one. Just one. |
| Generic stock-photo aesthetic | Data-report, technical-manual aesthetic |
| Blue as a background color | Blue as a highlight only |
| Rounded or serif fonts | Lexend Deca + JetBrains Mono only |
| "We" or corporate first-person plural | "I" — Anthony speaks directly |
