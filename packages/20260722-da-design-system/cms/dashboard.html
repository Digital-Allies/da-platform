Blog Post Structure

Headline: Clear, benefit-focused title
Intro: Hook the reader in first 2 sentences
Body: 3-5 main points with subheadings
Conclusion: Recap + CTA

Press Release Structure

Headline: News-focused, factual
Dateline: City, Date
Opening: Who, what, when, where, why
Details: Supporting facts and quotes
Boilerplate: Company info

Case Study Structure

Challenge: Client's problem
Solution: What we did
Results: Metrics and outcomes
Testimonial: Client quote
Next Steps: How to replicate
