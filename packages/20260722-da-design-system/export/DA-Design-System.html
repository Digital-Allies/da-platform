Components

Brand Marks

Pulse Dot
12px circle animated at 3000ms interval. Live connection indicator. Never used for decorative purposes.
Signal Dot
14px red circle at full opacity. POI marker or attention cue. Never animated.

Yellow card with signal-red border and pin. Used for callouts, warnings, and brand-voice highlights.

Brand

Voice & Tone

Digital Allies speaks with technical clarity, warm empathy, and midwest pragmatism. The voice bridges engineering rigor and human-centered problem-solving.

Primary Voice
Authoritative but warm. We speak as the expert in the room who genuinely wants to help. No corporate jargon. No condescension.
Example: "We take the chaos out of digital marketing so you can focus on what matters."
Tone Spectrum
Technical & Clear: For spec docs, API copy, developer-facing content.
Human & Direct: For marketing, case studies, audience-facing messaging.
Midwest Pragmatic: No-nonsense, grounded, respectful of the user's time.
Motion

Animation & Timing

Duration Use Case Timing Function
180ms (--dur-quick) Micro-interactions (hover, focus) cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
300ms (--dur-base) Standard transitions cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)
600ms (--dur-slow) Fade-ins, large movements cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)
3000ms (--dur-pulse) Pulse dot only ease-in-out

Philosophy: Motion is calm. The pulse dot is the only continuous animation. Prefer stillness to motion; motion should always serve purpose, never decoration.

Structural

Borders & Radius

The brand is corner-square. Corners are cut at 90°, with rare exceptions.

Token Value Use
