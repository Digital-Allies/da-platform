# DA Caption Guidelines

## Brand Voice Summary

Digital Allies speaks to small business owners in Mohave County (Kingman, Bullhead City, Lake Havasu City). The tone is direct, practical, and local — not corporate, not hype, not preachy. Every post earns its place with something real.

The one-liner test: if you can cut a sentence and the idea still lands, cut it.

## Meta Caption Format (FB + IG)

**Structure:**
```
[Hook — 1 punchy line]
[Supporting line or CTA — optional, 1–2 lines max]

→ [article URL]
```

**Rules:**
- Hook first — don't warm up, don't introduce
- Keep it to 1–3 total lines before the URL
- Article URL goes on its own line with → as the lead
- No hashtags unless Anthony specifically asks
- No emojis unless Anthony asks
- Local angle in the hook when it fits naturally — don't force it
- No exclamation points — the work should speak without them

**Good example:**
```
Repetitive tasks are for machines. You've got better things to do.

→ digitalallies.net/learn/self-governing-bureau
```

**Bad example:**
```
Hey Kingman! Are you tired of doing the same things over and over? 
Automation can help your business thrive! 🚀 Check out our new article!
#SmallBusiness #Automation #Kingman
```

## GBP Caption Format

**Structure:**
```
[Informational hook — what question does this answer?]
[Supporting sentence — real detail, not just restatement]

Read it: [article URL]
```

**Rules:**
- GBP captions are SEO signals — write for searchers, not scrollers
- State the topic plainly in sentence 1 (think: what would someone Google?)
- 2–3 sentences max
- Use "Read it: [URL]" — never "Learn more" or "Click here"
- No "Offer" post type — always "What's new"
- One post per article page — don't repeat GBP posts for the same article

**Good example:**
```
AI search is changing how customers find local businesses. Our latest article explains the difference between SEO and AEO — and why Mohave County businesses need both to stay visible.

Read it: digitalallies.net/learn/seo-aeo
```

## Carousel / Multi-Image Posts (Meta only)

For Kingman Data Story or future carousels:
- Cover slide = hook only, 1 line
- Slides 2–end: each slide has one data point or idea
- Caption: "Swipe through. [topic summary in 1 line].\n\n→ [URL if applicable]"

## Caption Length Reference

| Platform | Target length |
|---|---|
| Meta (FB+IG) | 1–3 lines + URL = ~50–100 chars total |
| GBP | 2–3 sentences = ~200–350 chars |
| IG Stories (if ever used) | 1 line max |

---

## What to avoid

- "We're excited to announce" — nobody cares
- Asking questions in the hook ("Did you know...?") — feels like content farm
- Stacking hashtags — not DA's style
- Explaining what the image shows — the image is self-evident
- Starting with the company name — put it at the end if at all
- Vague CTAs like "contact us today" — the URL is the CTA
