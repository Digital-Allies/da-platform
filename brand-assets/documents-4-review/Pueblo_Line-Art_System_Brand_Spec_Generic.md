# Pueblo Line-Art System — Brand Spec (Generic / Identifier-Free)

A Southwestern / Mexican-Pueblo architectural line-art design system. **All business names, personal names, addresses, phone numbers, and contact info removed.** Reusable as a generic design language.

## Color Palette

- **Primary Teal** `#008080` (deep variant `#006D77`) — structural borders, body copy, outlines
- **Terracotta Orange** `#C8643C` (approx) — accent strokes, prices, CTAs, roofs
- **Accent Pink** `#E84E8A` (approx) — organic petals, floating dots, highlights
- **Cream / Adobe** `#FDFBF7` — background, mimics natural adobe-paper texture

## Typography

- **Display/Headers:** elegant high-contrast serif (Pliay / Roslindale Display character) — for titles
- **Body/UI:** geometric sans-serif (DM Sans character), Deep Teal
- No drop shadows, glows, or WordArt effects

## Line-Art Rules

- **Monoline strokes:** Primary 2px, Detail 1px
- **Corner radius:** "softened geometric" — hand-cut paper feel
- High contrast, WCAG-friendly

## Architectural "Atomic Units"

- **The Geometry:** stepped ziggurat forms
- **The Structure:** concentric semi-circular arches
- **The Motif:** organic petals + floating dots
- Assemblies: **The Chapel** (vertical: arch entry, bell tower, cross), **The Plaza** (horizontal: wide central gate + flanking gates + patterned wall), **The Hacienda** (multi-story full-color hero building)

## Parrot Mascot System ("stickers" that break rigid grid)

- Green parrot in terracotta/yellow sombrero + red/green/yellow striped serape
- **The Greeter** — wings spread wide, inside an archway
- **The Guide** — striding pose, one wing raised
- **The Relaxer** — reclining, sleeping under tilted sombrero

## Background World

- **The Casitas:** small simplified isometric terracotta/cream houses
- **The Particles:** pink 4-pointed stars + teal open circles
- Grid system, parallax Z-depth (casitas sit furthest back)

## Logo / Construction Kit

- Square mark: grid of colored rectangles (teal, terracotta, white, pink) — geometric, text-free
- Decorative border strips: alternating terracotta/teal zigzag + petal
- Scalable language: solid ziggurat + outlined arch = infinite variations (e.g. patterned infinity symbol)
