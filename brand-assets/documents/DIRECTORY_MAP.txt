================================================================================
NAMING RULES
================================================================================

Campaigns:    YYYYMM-campaignname
              Example: 2026-06-design, 2026-07-automation
              NOT: design_june, june_2026_design, Design Campaign

Images:       001.png, 002.png, 003.png, etc.
              OR: 001-cover.png, 002-slide.png
              NOT: image1, design1, final_version_2_REAL

Dates:        YYYY-MM-DD (in JSON)
              Example: 2026-06-09
              NOT: 06/09/26, 9-june, tuesday

Platforms:    "meta" (Facebook + Instagram together)
              "gbp" (Google Business Profile)
              "all" (both)
              NOT: "facebook", "instagram", "Meta", "FB"

Status:       "pending", "posted", "archived"
              NOT: "todo", "done", "publishing"
