## Local Growth Program

Tiered website + digital presence packages for local businesses. All tiers include WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. Bilingual (EN/ES) is standard from Starter up.

| Tier | Description | Key Inclusions |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Starter** | Digital presence setup for local businesses ready to get found. | Mobile-first site (up to 5 pages), basic on-page SEO, GBP setup, 30-day post-launch check-in, WCAG 2.1 AA, EN/ES |
| **Growth** | A step up for businesses ready to grow their reach. | Up to 8 pages, AEO + SEO audit, GBP optimization, bilingual option, WCAG 2.1 AA — built to be found by people and AI |
| **Standard** | Full digital presence built for growth. | Up to 10 pages, AEO + SEO audit, monthly analytics, 1hr support/mo, bilingual, WCAG compliant, AI-discoverable content structure |
| **Full Deployment** | The full package for businesses ready to compete. | Everything in Standard + custom integrations (booking, POS, intake forms), workflow automation, 3hrs support/mo, quarterly content + SEO refresh, ongoing AEO monitoring |

## Key Service Notes

**Bilingual (EN/ES) is standard** — All Local Growth tiers include bilingual or bilingual option. The Command Center Alliance monthly hours can be used for bilingual content. EN/ES is the starting point; additional languages available as needed.

**WCAG 2.1 AA is standard** — All website and hosting products include WCAG-compliant infrastructure. Bilingual sites are part of accessibility strategy for businesses serving Spanish-speaking communities in Arizona.

**GBP is a service, not just a channel:**
- GBP setup: included in Local Growth Starter
- GBP optimization: included in Local Growth Growth
- Ongoing GBP management: available as part of Command Center Alliance monthly hours

**AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is a differentiator:**
- Included in Growth, Standard, Full Deployment
- Ongoing AEO monitoring in Full Deployment
- Content structured so AI assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.) can find and recommend the business

**The No-Ghosting Guarantee:**
- Explicitly mentioned in Command Center Alliance: "No ghosting. No ticket queue. You get a real person."
- Core differentiator across all plans.
