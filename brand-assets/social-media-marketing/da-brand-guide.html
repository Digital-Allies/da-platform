07 — Brand Voice
UseDirect, confident, human. Clear outcomes over jargon.
UseAction verbs. Short sentences. Measurable claims.
Use"We build" / "You get" framing — puts people in the story.
AvoidBuzzwords: synergy, leverage, solutions, robust, seamless.
AvoidVague promises or superlative claims without proof.
AvoidOver-formal tone — this is a human brand, not a bank.
08 — Motion & Graphic Language
Motion built from dot, line, and contrast — no decorative animation for its own sake
Kinetic compositions radiate from a single dot or point source
Use converging lines or expanding dot grids for backgrounds
Transitions: fast enter (200ms), slow exit (350ms). Ease-out preferred.
Photography: human, real, forward-looking. Authentic over polished.
09 — Usage Rules
Never recolor the blue dot to any other color
Never stretch, skew, or rotate the wordmark
Never place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a clear field
Never use the all-caps wordmark at display sizes — it's for small labels only
Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the dot on all sides
Minimum logo size: 80px wide in digital, 25mm in print
