Our Makers

Every shelf tells a story. Here's who's making what you find at West of 3rd.

EV
Elena Vasquez
Silversmith · Kingman

Hand-stamped silver and turquoise work, influenced by her grandmother's Diné jewelry traditions.

Small-batch dried herbs, teas, and wildcrafted plant medicine. Grown without synthetics, harvested by hand.
