Accessibility Notes

All color combinations meet WCAG AA contrast standards (4.5:1 minimum for normal text).

✓ Primary Text: Bone White (#f0e6d6) on Deep Charcoal (#1a1a1a) = 17.5:1 contrast

✓ Headlines: Celestial Yellow (#ffd966) on Deep Charcoal (#1a1a1a) = 9.8:1 contrast

✓ Secondary Text: Woven Moss (#a89a8a) on Deep Charcoal (#1a1a1a) = 6.5:1 contrast

✓ Link Text: Rattan Tan (#d4aa82) on Deep Charcoal (#1a1a1a) = 8.2:1 contrast

All combinations tested via WebAIM Contrast Checker. No color-only dependency; text + icon + context always provided.
