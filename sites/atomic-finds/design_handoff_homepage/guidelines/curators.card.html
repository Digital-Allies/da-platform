The Curators are Atomic Finds' lime-green mascot characters — playful stand-ins for discovery, wonder, curiosity, and character. They add visual lightness and point to the fun side of taking vintage seriously. They are illustrated mascots only; Jennyfer is the real person behind every restoration and sourcing decision — never use The Curators to imply otherwise.
